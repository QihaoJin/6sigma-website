About 6 Sigma

AI That Cares

We believe AI should enrich childhood — not replace it.
Technology that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and connection.

Our Mission

To create the safest, smartest, and most delightful AI companion for children — one that respects privacy, supports learning, and brings families closer together.

Our Story

6 Sigma Limited was founded in 2026 in Hong Kong by a team of AI researchers and parents who saw a gap between the promise of AI and products safe enough for children. We set out to build something we'd trust with our own kids.

Our Values

Privacy by Design

On-device AI from day one. Your child's data never leaves home.

Child-First Engineering

Every feature tested through child safety, developmental psychology, and plain fun.

Transparent & Honest

No hidden data collection. No manipulative design. We tell you exactly how it works.
